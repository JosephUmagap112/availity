Terminology Used

  • Authorization: Approval for a specific service.
  • Referral: Recommendation to another provider.
  • NPI: National Provider Identifier.
  • ICD-10: Diagnosis code.
  • CPT/HCPCS: Procedure code.
  • Place of Service: Location where service is provided.
  • Units: Number of services or items.
